About PcbMatic

PcbMatic is a Leading High Technology Printed Circuit Board supplier and service provider. We are specialized in Rigid PCB’s, Flexible, Rigid Flex , Metal Based and HDI PCB’s. We are strategic partners of demanding customers who face major challenges to design, manufacture and deliver their products, with the aim of bringing new technology solutions to transportation, entertainment, saving lives, connecting people, and more.

PcbMatic Pvt Limited is registered and started its activity in 2023 with the motto of supply the International quality standard Printed Circuit Boards.

PcbMatic is ISO 9001-2015 with the quality management System Certified company from Mysore (India).
